Quik Tix



Are you a host with an upcoming event? Can’t seem to get your tickets to all your customers? Look no further… Introducing the new and innovative app QuikTix!

Reduce the need for ticket outlets and personal distributors. Get your tickets straight to your phone! The QuikTix App allows your sales data to be recorded and analysed to optimize your profits and suit your business needs.

What is QuikTix?

QuikTix is an app designed to make attending events a much simpler task. In a perfect tech savvy world, there would be no need physical tickets and everyone could easily access applications on their devices. Since it’s not, our app’s goal is to greatly minimize the use of physical tickets. Our app would provide a platform for event planners and event goers to communicate seamlessly.
